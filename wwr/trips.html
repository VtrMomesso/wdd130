Best Trips

Waterfall in a Beautiful Place

In a refreshing waterfall with a beautiful landscape to enjoy the view and a great place to have a family picnic. Taking advantage of this moment to take lots of photos and then go down the wet water river.

Hidden Water Cave

A cave with crystal clear water, having the highest amount of mineral salts, making it pure water with a great effect on the immune system. With great possibility to hunting for food with our instructor, and going down on the most beautifull river.

Golden River in the Green Passage

There are many mysteries along these golden waters, and along the way some of them will be revealed for those of you looking for a great adventure with your family. However, among the greatest gifts, seeing the white alligator will make this experience unforgettable.
